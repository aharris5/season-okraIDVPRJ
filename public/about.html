About Indie Games and Indie Realm

Why did you start Indie Realm?

-We wanted to allow gamers to have a central spot in this vast internet where they could easily go to and find the best Indie games and support developers who don't use financials from a publisher. So the money goes directly to the man or team that created the game!

What is an Indie Game?

-An independent video game (commonly referred to as an indie game) is a game that is created without the financial support of a publisher. Basically, the developers use their own personal funds or crowdsource for starting capital in order to potentially hire a small team and begin development on their video game.
